About Us

We are the
Green Bridge for Kanpur

We are a youth-driven initiative working to reduce extreme heat in Kanpur through tree plantation, water relief, and community awareness. Every action we take is rooted in urgency and love for our city.

🌍
Our Vision
A cooler, greener Kanpur where every street is shaded and every resident thrives.
🎯
Our Mission
Mobilize youth to plant trees, install water stations, and educate communities on climate resilience.
🤝
Community
Partnering with schools, colleges, and local bodies to create lasting green impact at scale.
💧
Water Relief
Setting up Matka/Pyaau stations across Kanpur's hotspots to provide free drinking water.
Our Programs

What We Do

Five pillars of action, united by a single mission: making Kanpur cooler, greener, and more livable for everyone.

01 🌳
Tree Plantation Drives

Organized mass plantation drives across schools, parks, roadways, and empty plots throughout Kanpur's most heat-affected zones.

Flagship Program
02 💧
Water Stations (Matka / Pyaau)

Installing earthen water pots and cooling stations at key locations to provide free drinking water to commuters, workers, and animals.

Active Year-Round
03 🐦
Bird Feeding Campaigns

Distributing bird feeders and setting up feeding stations to support urban biodiversity and raise public awareness about wildlife.

Biodiversity
04 🌡️
Urban Cooling Awareness

Street plays, social media campaigns, workshops, and door-to-door outreach to educate citizens about reducing urban heat island effect.

Community Education
🏫
05
Campus Collaborations

We actively partner with schools, colleges, and universities to run green clubs, plantation events, and eco-awareness programs that inspire the next generation of climate champions.

Schools · Colleges · Universities
